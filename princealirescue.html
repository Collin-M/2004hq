2004HQ - Prince Ali Rescue

Description:Prince Ali of Al Kharid has been kidnapped by the scheming Lady Keli. You are hired to stage a rescue mission

Difficulty Level: Easy

Items/Skills Needed To Start:

Rope: You can buy from Ned in Draynor Village.

Wig: Bring four balls of wool to Ned, he'll make you a wig.

Yellow dye: Bring two onions and 5 coins to aggie (onions near Fred the farmers house.)

Red Berries: You can find some near the Varrock mining area.

Two buckets of water: Get an empty bucket (you can buy from shops) and use it on a sink, fountain or well.

Flour: You need a pot and wheat, you can find wheat in a field east from Fred the farmers house. Bring the wheat to the windmill at the top floor use it with the hopper then operate the hopper, once your at the first floor your flour will be in the barrel.

Ashes: Make a fire, once it burns out you get ashes.

Pink Skirt: Buy at Varrock clothes shop.

Three beers: Buy at any bar that sells "beer".

Soft clay: Mine clay and use water on clay.

Bronze bar: Mine one tin ore and one copper ore smelt them and you get a bronze bar.

Starting Location:Talk to Hassan in the Al Kharid palace

Reward:3 quest points, 700gp, permission to pass thru the Al Kharid gate for free

Start the quest by talking to Hassan in the palace, he'll tell you to speak with Osman.

Tell him Hassan sent you, and you need instructions, he'll tell you Lady Keli has been captured and you need to make a rescue, he'll then tell you that you need to do two things.One of them is to make a disguise and the other to make an imprint of a key.

Head over to Draynor Village, find Leela you'll tell her you are there to help her free the prince, then she'll check what items you have and tell you what else you still need.

Find Aggie the witch, she can make you the skin paste and yellow dye.

Talk to need, bring him 4 balls of yarn and he can make a rope and with 3 balls of wool he can make you a wig for the disguise.

Talk to Lady Keli, tell her you've heard of her, shes famous in all of runescape. Then ask her what her latest plan is, then tell her she must have been very skillful. Then ask her if shes sure they wont let the prisnor out. Make sure you have the soft clay here and ask her if you can see the key..then ask her if you can touch it for a moment then you'll make an imprint of the key in the clay.

Go back to Osman in Al Kharid with the key imprint & a bronze bar, he'll tell you to pick up the key from Leela

Go into the house nearby Lady Keli and talk to the guard tell him you have a beer, fancy one? You'll then ask him if he wants another and automatically hand them to him, he will drink them and get drunk.

Tie up lady keli, use your key on the door and talk to the prince he will take the disguise and run off..congratz! your now a friend of Al Kharid and can pass for free.
