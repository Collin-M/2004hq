2004HQ - Elemental Workshop

Description: Hundreds of years ago a mineral was found that had the ability to change the property of magic.

The magicians, fearing the effect this may have on their profession, sealed this workshop forever; or so they had hoped. See if you can rediscover the lost knowledge of elemental ore.

Difficulty Level: Medium

Length: Short

Requirements: 20 Mining, 20 Smithing, and 20 Crafting

Items Needed: A sharp object like a knife, sword, etc. Pickaxe, Leather, Thread, Needle, 4 Coal, and a Hammer.

Quest Points Reward: 1

Reward: Element Shield (+6 Magic in Defence bonus), 5000 XP in Crafting, 5000 XP in smithing.

Start Point: Go to Seers' Village.

To Start: Search the bookcase on the east wall in house southwest of the bank in Seers' village.

Instructions:

Go to the house southwest of the bank in Seers' village.



Search the bookcase on the east wall.



After you read the Battered book, use your knife on the book and you will get a Battered key. Do not drop the Battered book, you will need it later to make the Elemental Shield.

Go to the building north which has a anvil in it and use the key on the Odd Looking Wall. Then go down the stairs.



A map of the places for some of the next steps:



When you're in, go down the west path and try and mine a rock. You will be attacked by an level 35 Earth elemental. Kill the Earth elemental and it will drop an Elemental ore, pick it up.



Now go to the north passage from where you came in, turn the valves so there are like this. First you need to turn the right valve then the left valve to make water wheel move. Then pull the lever. (Right valve is the first and the second is the left valve).



After that go to the east passage and fix the bellows. This is where the leather, thread and needle comes in.





After that search all the creates for A stone bowl (you will only need to search each crate once). The location is different for each player. When you find it go down the south passage.



Use the bowl on the lava and you will get A stone bowl filled with lava. Then use the bowl with lava in it on the furnace.



Go back to the bellows and pull the switch.

Go back to the furnace and use your Elemental ore and 4 coal on the furnace.



Then go back to the center and use your Elemental metal on the Workbench. You will need to have the Battered book in your inventory to make the shield or it will tell you that you need instructions.



When you've made the Elemental shield you've completed the quest.

Quest Complete

Note: You can make more Elemental shields after you've finished the quest.
