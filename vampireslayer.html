2004HQ - Vampire Slayer

Description: Count Draynor is terrorizing the small village of Draynor. The townsfolk want you to kill this vampire.

Difficulty Level: Medium

Items/Skills Needed To Start: A hammer, decent armor and weapon, a few gp to buy some beers.

Starting Location: Draynor Village

Reward: 3 quest points, some attack and defense xp

Instructions:

Find Morgan in Draynor, its not that hard, its just a small village. He'll tell you about the vampire and that he wants you to kill it. He'll also tell you to go find his friend Dr. Harlow at the Jolly Boar Inn.

First, go upstairs and search the wall cupboard for a clove of garlic. Then go north-east of varrock to the Inn.

Talk to him and get him a beer that he'll ask for. He'll tell you how to beat it and give you a stake.

Get everything you need and go to Draynor Manor. Head to the stairs that go down. Open the coffin and the Count should jump out and start attacking you.

Attack him until he's full red bar and the stake should automatically stab him. If not make sure you have a hammer with you and stake. If it still doestn't work try stuff like using garlic on him and manually stabbing him with stake.

Once he's dead you have finished the quest (Congratuations)
