2004HQ - Tribal Totem

Description: Lord Handelmort of Ardougne is a collector of exotic artifacts. A recent addition to his private collection is a strange looking totem from Karamja. The Rantuki tribe are not happy about the recent disappearance of their totem.

Difficulty Level: Easy

Items/Skills Needed To Start: 21 thieving +coins for travelling to Karamja.


Starting Location: Brimhaven.

Reward: 1 quest point, approx. 1250 thieving experience, and 5 Cooked Swordfish

Talk to Kangai in the Shrimp and Parrot pub. He will say that his totem was stolen by Lord Handelmort.


Go to the house that has a garden,the house with gardener is just west of ardounge market, with the gaurd dogs in there. talk to the gardener and he will tell you about the security of the house.Try to open the door,you will find that you can't go in by the front door.


Go and talk to Wizard Cromperty who is in a house in blue colour in northeast of Ardogine.Talk to him and ask him to teleport you,you will be teleported in the RPDT Depot.


Search a box that will be delievered to the mansion.You will get a label.Use the label on the next box which will be sent to the wizard tower and talk to the RPDT employee and ask him to deliever the box.The boxes will be delievered.


Search the houses in northwest,one of them has a guide book inside.Pick it up and read it.


Go back to the house where Wizard Cromperty is.Ask him to teleport you again.This time you will be teleported inside the mansion.


Pick the lock on the door.You will need to type a passsword.The password is K U R T(not B R A D).and the door will open.Search the trap on the stairs and go up.


Open the chest and search it.You will get a totem.


Return to Kangai and he will reward you.
