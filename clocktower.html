2004HQ - Clock Tower

Description: Help brother Kojo to fix his clock tower by finding the missing cogs.

Difficulty Level: Medium

Items/Skills Needed To Start: Bucket of water or ice gloves, and a good weapon and armour since you need to get pass some ogres - lvl 53.

Starting Location: South West of Ardougne Zoo

Reward: 1 quest point and 500 coins.

Instructions:

Talk to Brother Kojo, he should be in clock tower just south west of Ardounge Zoo.

You need to get all four cogs, remember you can only carry one at the time.

Go down ladder and you should be in a dungeon, In next room you will see colored blocks on the floor represent the general direction of the cog.

Black Cog

Use the colored block to see where to go. Go down the pass to a door. Behind the door you will see lvl 2 giant spiders and the black cog surrounded by fires.





Use the bucket of water or wear your ice gloves and pick it up. Now go back to the ladder up to the first floor.

At the first floor use your black cog on the black pole.

Red Cog

Go to the colored bricks in the next room and go through the door. The red brick represents south east.

Go through the door and you get to a room with three ogre's which are level 53 and the red cog.





Go back to the ladder, go up to the 2nd floor and use red cog on the red pole.

White Cog

Go down the ladder to the dungeon and go to the room with the colored bricks. Now go through the door where white brick represents north west.

Continue going north west to get the rat poison and after you have picked up rat poison go east to the rat cage.

At the rat cage you will see two levers to open the two doors,pull the levers to get into cage.





Inside the cage you will see the rats food trough. Use the rat poison on it and the rats will go to eat from it and die. Then the room with the white cog will open.





Pick up the white cog and go up the ladder, and you will appear outside of the clock tower. Go inside and use the white cog on the white pole near the ladder, on the 3rd floor.

Blue Cog

Go down to the dungeon and to the room with the colored bricks. Go through the door where the blue brick represents south west.

After going in you will get to a room with some monsters locked in cages, but sadly the blue cog is locked in also with no doors. So you will need to find an entrance to the blue cog.





Go up the ladder in the room and you will appear outside the clock tower. Now go north east to Brother Cedric is, just west of him is a ladder.





Go down the ladder and just follow the passage to a wall. Go through the door and pick up the blue cog. Go up the ladder and go inside the clock tower, up the staircase to 2nd floor and use blue cog on blue pole.

Now that all of the cogs have been placed talk to Brother Kojo to receive your reward.
